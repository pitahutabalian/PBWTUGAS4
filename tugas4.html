Welcome to Our Website

The Pyramid Game tradition has been going on for years at Baekyeon High School. The students at the top of the pyramid have power over those at the bottom. They often carry out bullying and violence against those who are considered "unpopular". Pyramid Game has a terrible impact on the students. Those who are victims of bullying experience trauma and depression. Some of them even committed suicide.
